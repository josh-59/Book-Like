About

Seems like there's a thousand CSS Stylesheets available on Github. This one is not different. It's just mine and I sort of like it. Its navigation is pretty fly; it's super-modern and might be good for documentation.

I'll admit, it's not the easiest to use. In particular, each page-navigation must be hard-coded into each other page. But for stable content like books, it's reasonable. You could even incorporate typeset math with Mathjax! And, everything's done up in div's, which seemed like a good idea at the time.

Enjoy!
